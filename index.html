Are you Missing someone?

Many people are not attending or able to attend GPN because of different barriers and missing structures, like:

  • missing safer spaces
  • lack of effective and easy Awareness structures
  • protection of perpetrators
  • Unsuitable or not easy usable area for wheelchair users
  • language barrier
  • and more…

Are you someone or do you know someone, who could not attend GPN because of anything like that?
This Form is for you!

We want to make ourselves visible.
We are not here in physical form. Some of us would have loved to join.
