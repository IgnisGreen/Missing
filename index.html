Are you Missing someone?

Many people are not attending/able to attend GPN because of many different barriers and missing structures, like:

  • missing safer spaces
  • lack of effective and easy Awareness structures
  • protection of perpetrators
  • Unsuitable/not easy usable area for wheelchair users
  • And many many more…

Are you someone or do you know someone, who could not attend GPN because of anything like that?
This Form is for you! Let's show us and how many we are.
We are not here in physical form. Many people going to events forget how many we are, those who could not go. But we exist!

Do NOT fill this form for others. Send it to them if you know someone <3 Everyone has to fill the form themselves.
It is okay and welcomed to fill in every space with anonymous, if you do not want to share that information about yourself :)
